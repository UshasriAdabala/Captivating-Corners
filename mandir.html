JAG MANDIR PALACE

Also known as 'Lake Garden Palace', this floating marvel will take your breath away with its magnificent architecture and gorgeous setting in the middle of the Lake Pichola.

State

Udaipur,Rajasthan

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Jag Mandir Palace is between October and March when the weather is pleasant and comfortable.During the summer months, it can get quite hot, so itâ€™s better to avoid visiting during that time.

What To Expect

Enjoy the idyllic setting and step back in time as you explore the splendid structures and expansive gardens. There's also an in-house restaurant and a museum here.

TIPS

  • Do carry a good camera if you want to capture the delightful lake views.
  • It is advisable to bring your own food and beverages as the charges here a little higher.
  • Sunset is the best time to enjoy the golden hues of the palace.
  • Don't forget to wear a life jacket if you're taking a boat ride till Jag Mandir Island Palace.
