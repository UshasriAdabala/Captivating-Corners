GREEN VALLEY

Green Valley is a popular photograph point flaunting natural beauty. The hills around this region are lined by rapturous deodar and pine trees.

State

Shimla, Himachal Pradesh

Best Time to Visit

February to April and from July to October when the fresh bloom of the forest is too tempting, flourishing with all the lush-greenery and natural vegetation.

What To Expect

Check out amazing nature views, and postcard-like mountain ranges, lush greenery, and snow-capped Himalayas.

TIPS

  • Carry your own binoculars for amazing views, or you can also buy a pair of binoculars available locally there.
  • Do carry some warm clothes, as it may get chilly in the evening.
  • Since the climb can be steep, it is advised to hire an experienced driver for safety.
