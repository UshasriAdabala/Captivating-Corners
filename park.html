BRYANT PARK

Sprawling in an expanse of 20.5 acres, this park was established by H. D. Bryant in 1908. The park houses over 325 species of trees, cacti and shrubs.

State

Bryant Park is located in the city of Kodaikanal, Tamil Nadu, India

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Bryant Park is during the months of April to June as well as September and October.

What To Expect

The park appears like a gorgeous rainbow when all its flowers bloom. There is a special section of roses, where you can find approximately 740 of its varieties.

TIPS

  • Check out the 157-year old Eucalyptus tree here.
  • Every summer, flower shows and horticultural exhibits are organised in the park.
  • The park remains open from 9:30 a.m. to 5:00 p.m.
