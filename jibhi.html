JIBHI

Surrounded by lush greenery, Jibhi is a tranquil, unexplored town in the Banjar Valley of Himachal Pradesh. Ideal for travellers seeking solace in the arms of nature, this quaint town provides breath-taking views perfect for outdoor activities like fishing, hiking, bird watching and more.

State

Manali,Himachal Pradesh

Best Time to Visit

March to May when most parts of India starts to get the summer heat and that is when people start looking for an escapade to beat the heat. The weather in Jibhi during summer stays pleasant throughout the day and chilly at night.

What To Expect

Explore the magical Jalori Pass, unwind in the calm of the Serolsar Lake nearby and relax by the Jibhi Waterfall, for a serene escape. Marvel at the commanding hill views from the Chehni Kothi, explore the Pahadi-style Chehni Fort and offer your prayers at the famous Krishna Temple here. For an immersive experience, go fishing in the many lakes, take long nature-walks and enjoy family picnics.

TIPS

  • Plan your stay in advance at guesthouses or tented eco-camp sites
  • Hire a car to get around the area for a hassle-free trip
  • The best time to visit is during March – May (summer), June – September (monsoon) & October – November (autumn)
  • The closest airport is Bhuntar in Kullu. You can also drive down or take the train from Shimla, or take the bus to Manali & disembark at Atuk
  • Visit the Shringa Rishi Temple for meditation & Raghupur Fort for a dose of history
  • Visit Kullu for adventure activities & the Great Himalayan National Park, about an hour away from Jibji, for the lush flora & fauna
