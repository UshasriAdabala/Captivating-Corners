ELEPHANT BEACH

Dotted with fascinating coral reefs, the unspoilt beach is a hotspot for water sports. The beautiful beach will steal your heart with its rich and alluring marine life.

State

Havelock Island

Best Time to Visit

Visiting Elephant Beach during the winter season Between November to March is also considered to be the best time to visit.

What To Expect

Capture gloriously golden sunset views and indulge in water sports like snorkelling, sea walking, scuba diving, and boating. Don't forget to go for the exclusive glass boat rides here!

TIPS

  • Carry an extra set of clothes if you want to sign up for water sports here.
  • Do try the lip-smacking seafood served at the swanky beach shacks.
  • Do not consume a heavy meal right before indulging in water sports.
